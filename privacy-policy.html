👋 Introduction

GridZen ("we", "our", or "us") is a free block puzzle mobile game developed by Ritesh Kadtan. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. GridZen is designed to be played completely offline and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

By using GridZen, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

📊 Information We Collect

Information stored on your device (locally):

  • Your chosen player name and avatar
  • Game progress (level, stars, scores)
  • Coin balance and hint count
  • Daily streak and play dates
  • Sound/music preferences

✅ All of the above is stored only on YOUR device using browser localStorage. We never transmit this data to any server. We cannot see it. Nobody else can see it.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your real name or email address
  • ❌ Phone number or contacts
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Camera or microphone access
  • ❌ Photos or files
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)
  • ❌ Browsing history

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

GridZen displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the game free for everyone.

Google AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier on your device)
  • General location (country/region level only)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

📋 This data is collected by Google, not by us. Google's data practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy:
policies.google.com/privacy

Types of ads shown in GridZen:

  • Banner ads (shown during gameplay)
  • Interstitial ads (shown between levels)
  • Rewarded ads (optional — watch to earn hints)

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

👶 Children's Privacy

GridZen is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used GridZen, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

GridZen does not contain content inappropriate for children, but due to advertising, we recommend parental supervision for younger users.

💾 Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard browser localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your game data
  • Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
  • Clearing app data in device settings resets the game

We do not operate any servers that store your personal information.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Your game data is always accessible to you on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall the app or clear app data to delete everything
  • Opt-out of ads: Use your device's ad personalization settings
  • Reset advertising ID: Available in your device's Google settings

🔐 App Permissions

GridZen requests only the minimum permissions needed:

  • ✅ Internet access — Required to load the game and show ads
  • ✅ Network state — To check connectivity for ads

GridZen does NOT request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other sensitive device features.

🌍 GDPR & CCPA Compliance

GridZen complies with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation, EU) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) privacy regulations.

EU Users (GDPR): You have the right to access, correct, delete, and restrict processing of your personal data. Since GridZen stores all data locally on your device and we collect no personal data on our servers, you have full control at all times.

California Users (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale. GridZen does not sell personal data. Ad-related data is handled by Google AdMob under their own policies.

Data Retention: Game data is stored locally on your device and retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data. We retain no data on our servers as we do not operate any.
